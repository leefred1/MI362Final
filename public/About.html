About Me...

My name is Fred Lee.

I am a Korean American and my birthday is on September 27th, 1995. My parents were born in Korea, but I was born in America. I have one little brother that is 7 years younger than I am. Fun Fact: the age gap between me and my brother is the same as the age gap between my parents!

I'm currently working on my Bachelor's degree for Computer Science and Engineering at Michigan State University. On the surface, it seems as if I'm studying to try and earn a stable living.

BUT I want to do much more than that.

I am a video game fanatic. But not just any video game fanatic, but an extremely competitive video game fanatic. What you see above is the stadium where the grand finals of a video game tournament were held. My dream is to be a part of this phenomenon called E-Sports. It matters not whether I contribute as a competitor, creator, or supporter; my dream is to witness E-Sports being held in the same regard as regular sports.

On a side note, my favorite food is Korean. Specifically Korean BBQ.

Sushi gets a very close second.
